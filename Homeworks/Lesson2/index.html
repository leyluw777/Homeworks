Best Switzerland Tours

Geneva tour

This 2-hour tour will show you the main sights of Switzerland’s second-largest city, including the United Nations Offices, the Jet d’Eau fountain, and the flower clock, among others.A friendly and knowledgeable guide will inform you everything you want to know while walking and riding the city’s tramway. Guided tours are available not only in English, but French too.

Titlis Full-Day Tour

This 5-and-a-half-hour tour will allow you to get acquainted with the magical winter wonderland Titlis glacier, no matter what time of year you visit. You’ll be taken on a stunning drive through Alpine scenery on a coach to Titlis mountain before boarding a rotating cable car, offering you the best views of your surroundings.Sleep off all the fun on the afternoon coach journey back to Lucerne.

View prices & reviews

James Bond “Goldeneye” Bungee Jump

An activity only for the bravest and most daring of tourists, this tour will allow you to jump into Pierce Brosnan’s shoes and replicate his jump from the Contra Dam, one of the largest in the world. The dam, located in the Italian speaking part of southern Switzerland isn’t only famous because of James Bond, but it has featured in a lot of other TV shows and films too.

Mt. Pilatus by Cable Car

This tour to one of Switzerland’s most beautiful mountains is a great summer day out, which will see you ride a cable car, take the steepest train in Europe, and relax on an hour-long lake cruise. After a short journey from Lucerne to Kriens in the morning, you’ll take a cable car whilst admiring views of the mountains from the panoramic windows. If you’re feeling adventurous, hike to the mountain peak.
